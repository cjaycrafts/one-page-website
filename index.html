Our Story

We started with nothing. No budget. No coding skills. Just three friends, too much soda, and a broken square that barely moved across the screen. It crashed constantly. But when that square finally bounced without breaking? We were hooked. We kept failing, fixing, and learning. Late nights, bad code, cold pizza, and one tiny room. Now we build real games that people actually enjoy. We're not a big studio. We're just builders who refused to quit. And we want to build something great — with you.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: create honest, fun, original games without corporate fluff or greedy monetization. We believe games should respect your time and your wallet. Beyond that, we want to help players discover indie gems while giving small developers a real place to grow, learn, and collaborate. No ego. No nonsense. Just good games made by people who actually care about the craft. If you love playing or making games for the right reasons — you belong here.

Our Vision

We envision a world where small teams and solo creators don't need a million dollars or a big publisher to succeed. Our vision is to become the go-to home for passionate developers who want to turn raw ideas into playable realities. A space where creativity beats budget, where players find fresh voices, and where every good game gets a fair shot. No gatekeepers. No red tape. Just a growing community of builders and players making something worth playing.
